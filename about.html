About me

I studied law in my country, South Korea. (Yonsei University) I went to law school in U.S. too. (Washington University in St. Louis) I practiced law in a small law firm in New York and Atlanta, GA. While I was practicing law, I thought about doing a different thing. Practing law was full of stress. I also found out that I am not so good at dealing with people. Furthermore, I read articles about artificial intelligence everyday. Someday, AI will replace human attorneys. After I quit, I decided to dive into software industries. I am still learning, but everyday my life is full of fun. Learning new things is not like studyingk but more like playing. I can create new things with my own hand, on my laptop.
